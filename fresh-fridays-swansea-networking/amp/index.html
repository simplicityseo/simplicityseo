Fresh Fridays – A Fresh Approach To Swansea Networking.

Peter Griffiths
3 weeks ago

On the last Friday of each month at Brewstones, Uplands at 5 pm there’s a free networking event. We’ve teamed up with The Wave to bring you a networking event like no other, Fresh Fridays.

At Simplicity SEO Swansea we’re not just stuck behing the computer in our office all the time (Just most of the day).

We love to network too and meet business owners from around the Swansea area.

When The Wave approached us with the idea we thought it would be a great event and we didn’t want to pass up on the opportunity.

You might have heard about this on The Wave, and at Simplicity SEO we’re excited to meet you all in a relaxed way that’s a little different from your standard networking event.

Relax and meet business owners over a drink listening to live music.

Brewstone offer boutique beer, expertly made cocktails and a vast selection of wines for you to enjoy whilst networking.

We hope to see you at the next Fresh Friday!
